Static Websites : These are basic websites with fixed content that rarely changes. They are simple to create and cost-effective but lack dynamic features.
Dynamic Websites : Dynamic websites use databases to retrieve and display content dynamically. They are ideal for sites that require frequent updates, such as news portals or e-commerce platforms.
